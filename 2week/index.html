3 types of tacos

Pastor

the taco al pastor is one of the most popular tacos in the metropolitan area of Mexico, its main characteristic is that it is made in a spinning top and is cheap for the consumer

Asada

The taco of Asada is typical of the northern zone of Mexico, it is made with charcoal and can be any type of meat but the most used are cow cuts like beef stake, decimillo, top sirlon, arrachera or even pork chops.

Barbacoa

the taco of barbacoa is native to the state of Hidalgo, this is made of lamb meat which is seasoned and cooked for approximately 12 hours, it is somewhat expensive but the meat is really soft and delicious.
